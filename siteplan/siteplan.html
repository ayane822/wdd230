Site purpose

The site's purpose is to provide people with English opportunities. The business has a lot of kinds of "English Learning." You can find anything that you want to be such as teachers, students, teachers and students, internships, and so on. Our purpose is to connect people and people through online English conversations all over the world.

Target udience

This site is for all people who want to teach English or learn English. If you are a native English speaker, you could be a teacher and help a lot of people. If you speak English as a second language and feel like you would like to improve more, you can have many kinds of lessons. There is also an internship opportunity in the Philippines. In that case, you can have lessons for free as you work with us.
