Repetition

Memmo app

https://memoo.app/

This website represents repetition because they are repeating a single element many times in a design. In each box, there are the same color, fonts, lines, and sizes. It helps users understand how interactive elements work.

White Space and Clean Design

SHINK inc.

https://shink-jp.com/

This website represents "White Space and Clean design" because they have empty space around the content and functional elements of a page. It is effective because it doesn't overwhelm the designs and the contents. In addition to this, white space lets your designs breathe by reducing the amount of text and functional elements that users see at once.

Rule of Thirds

Amazon

amazon.com

This website represents "Rule of Thirds" because the main design which is two persons in the picture places in the right third of the images. It is also an essential way to express because it helps draw the viewer's eye into the image and places more emphasis on the subject.
